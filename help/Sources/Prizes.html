How to claim prizes

Depending on whether you have claimed a line or a bingo, you can win several different prizes. For every line you claim you get a prize, and if you get a bingo you obtain 3 prizes. To redeem them click on the items you want for every prize you have won. When you have selected all you can click the Redeem button to get them. If you change your mind over an item you can click again on it to deselect it
